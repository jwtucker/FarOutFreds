Fredburgers $5 or 2/$8

Chopped up & twisted, these are not your regular burgers! Served open face.

Fred's Original This one is where it all started! Fred's Favorite is his take on a pizza burger. Topped with colby cheese. Add chopped pepperoni for an extra kick!

America Fred's take on the classic bacon cheeseburger. Topped with shredded lettuce, chopped tomato, minced onion, dill pickle relish & our delicious All-American sauce.

Greece Our Greek burger encapsulates many familiar Mediterranean flavors and serves it up in a new & unique way. We top it off with an olive tapenade, chopped pepperoncini and tzatziki sauce.

Cuba Try a classic Cuban sandwich twisted up with a Fredburger! Burger, ham, cheese, topped with dill pickle relish and yellow mustard. It's a yummy twist on a classic that you won't find anywhere else!

Korea Take a quick trip to the orient with this original burger. A mash-up of succulent ground black angus & pork. Korean BBQ, topped with cheese, chopped Kim Chi (a spicy fermented cabbage) & our own Korean spicy drizzle.

Jamaica A Jerk Chicken Fredburger?! You bet, and you'll taste all of the tropics — an original mix — topped with cheese, sautéed peppers, onions & our own Jamaican sauce.

New This Year

India (vegetarian) For all you veggie lovers out there, Fred could no longer ignore the requests! Black bean, sautéed mushrooms, green peppers, tofu, and lots of Indian spice. Topped with fresh arugula & a toasted housemade goat cheese spread.

England Our delicious organic, grass fed, black angus burger and ground lamb get twisted up with our own original shepherd pie filling. Topped with colby cheese, toasted mashed potatoes & house-made golden onion drizzle!

Mexico Our Mexico burger honors so many great Mexican flavors, topped with cheese, roasted poblano pepper, shredded lettuce, chopped tomato, sour cream & housemade verde sauce.

Italy Burger & Italian sausage mashed up with delicious marinara. Topped with Provolone cheese, sautéed peppers, onions & a fresh marinated Italian salsa!

Other Menu Items

Risotto Balls Crispy on the outside — warm, cheesy, gooey goodness on the inside — Available in two flavors, bacon/cheddar & spicy. Try our housemade Sriracha mayo for dipping or play it safe with buttermilk ranch. Extra sauces 75¢ $3 small/$5 large

Marinated Beef Kabobs Beautiful organic grass-fed black angus beef marinated & grilled to perfection to bring out all the flavor. Served w/flavorful housemade chimichurri sauce for dipping. $9

Dessert Cinnamon rolls, new desserts coming soon! $3

We will always have at least 4 of these burgers on our menu at each event depending on seasonality. The Original & America will always be available!
